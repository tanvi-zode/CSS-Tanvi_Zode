Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Instructions:

1. Cook bacon in a skillet until crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.

2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.

3. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of a bread slice, followed by 2 slices of bacon, tomato slices, and another slice of cheese. Top with another slice of bread, buttered side up.

4. Repeat with remaining ingredients to make 2 sandwiches.

5. Jeat a skillet over medium heat and cook sandwiches until golden brown on both sides and cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.

6. Serve hot and enjoy!
